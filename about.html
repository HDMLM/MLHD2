About MLHD2

Our Project

MLHD2 is a lightweight, privacy-first logging tool for HELLDIVERS 2™ that helps you log missions, summarize results, and share highlights easily. Whether you want to track your best runs or keep a clean record of your progress. It provides you with the necessities for logging and sharing your missions without overwhelming you with unnecessary features.

We Guarantee

  • Simple to use before and after missions
  • Insightful summaries without noise
  • Keep your data local and in your control at all times
  • Open source: inspect, build, and modify the code to verify and control how MLHD2 works
  • Free and with no ads (except for those that spread managed democracy)
  • Respectful performance and reliability

The Team

MLHD2 is developed by two passionate gamers and developers who love HELLDIVERS 2™ and value privacy. We created MLHD2 to enhance our own gaming experience and share it with the community. We are committed to maintaining and improving MLHD2 based on user feedback and evolving needs while respecting your privacy and data security.

We are not affiliated with Arrowhead Game Studios or Sony Interactive Entertainment.
