Privacy

MLHD2 is built to be privacy-first. Your logs stay on your machine unless you choose to export them.

What we collect

MLHD2 does not collect, store, or transmit personal information. The app runs entirely on your device and your mission logs remain local unless you choose to export them. The code is open-source on GitHub for verification.

  • No personal data, device information, or usage statistics are collected.
  • No background telemetry or network calls for data collection.
  • No cookies, web beacons, or tracking technologies.

Third-party services

MLHD2 integrates with Microsoft Excel and Discord to provide core functionality. These services may process data under their own privacy policies; review them to understand how your information may be handled when using those integrations.

Data sharing

Because MLHD2 does not collect data, nothing is shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties.

If this policy changes, we will document the behavior here.

Your control

  • Delete logs at any time from within the app.
  • Export only what you want to share.
  • No background telemetry.
  • Open source: inspect, build, and modify the code to verify and control how MLHD2 works.
