Our Mission

We want to provide an easy to understand and immersive form for Helldivers, cadets and veterans alike, the opportunity to be able to look back on their progress and highest achievements within their journey in the Galactic War, by saving more advanced data than what is provided to you in game.

We believe that this program is a missed opportunity for what could be an amazing in-game feature, and we're confident that we have achieved this goal outside of the game.

Why MLHD2?

Logging your Helldivers 2 missions should be simple and efficient. MLHD2 is designed to minimize distractions and maximize insights, so you can focus on what matters: your gameplay. There are many features our project provides that isn't available in game and goes further in-depth, maximizing your potential through analytics and being easy to show off to your friends with ease!

This is a much more resource and storage efficient alternative to saving clips and videos of your experiences, although visually media can capture your moments a lot better than words can, however we understand that with exceptional quality videos, even in short form, can come with intensive resource consumption and large storage sizes.

Our program allows you to record your missions in a text based format, that is easy to read and understand, while also being able to export your data in a variety of formats, whether it's our Discord Embed system or the use of Markdowns and JSON, allowing you to use your locally stored data in other applications and services.

Why Now?

It's never too late to start logging your progress, you can treat it like a brand new journey! Using our program, from this moment onward, you can log every single mission that you do without the regret of not being able to look back on your works. Due to HELLDIVERS 2™ being a live service game, there's always going to be something new in the future, there's no harm in starting now!

Besides IT'S FREE! The sooner you start, the more data you'll have to analyze and share with your friends. Don't miss out on the opportunity to enhance your Helldivers 2 experience with MLHD2!
